Project Structure — What Goes Where
📂 assets/ — all your source files (INPUT)
  📂 loops/ — video loops organized by category
    📂 slime/ — put .mp4 loop files here
      📄 mat2_scene_1.mp4, mat2_scene_2.mp4 ...
  📂 music/ — music tracks organized by mood
    📂 ambient/ — calm music
    📂 epic/ — energetic music
    📂 winter/ — seasonal
  📂 fonts/ — custom fonts (Poppins-Bold.ttf)

📂 out/ — all generated content (OUTPUT)
  📂 renders/ — final .mp4 videos
  📂 thumbs/ — auto-generated thumbnails .jpg

📂 templates/ — JSON configs
  📄 satisfying.json — main template (duration, category, variants)
  📄 hooks.json — text overlay phrases
